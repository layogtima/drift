Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

We get it, privacy policies are usually about as fun as watching paint dry.

But since you're drifting into interesting corners of the internet, you deserve to know what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Spoiler: we're not creepy about it.

What We Collect (No Account)

Your drift history - Which sites you've visited via Drift stored locally on your device
Like/dislike preferences - Your thumbs up/down clicks local-only
Basic stats - Total drifts, likes, dislikes local

None of this leaves your browser. We can't see it even if we wanted to.

If You Create an Account (Optional)

Only needed if you want to submit URLs to share with others:

Email address - For authentication and account recovery
Username - Your chosen display name
Submitted URLs - Sites you suggest for the collection

What We DON'T Collect

Browsing history outside Drift
Page content or behavior
Your location
Financial information
Health data
Keystrokes or mouse tracking

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties.

We do not share your data except with our API server (drift.surf) to sync submitted URLs and account info, or if legally required by valid court order.

Your Rights

Delete your data - Uninstall the extension and poof, local data is gone. For account data, email us at drift@codeuncode.com
Request a copy of what we have
Opt out anytime - Just don't create an account and everything stays local
